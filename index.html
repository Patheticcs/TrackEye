Frequently Asked Questions

How does TrackEye protect user privacy?

TrackEye is designed with privacy in mind. We never capture personally identifiable information (PII) unless explicitly configured to do so. We're fully GDPR compliant and provide tools for consent management and data anonymization.

Can I integrate TrackEye with my existing systems?

Yes! TrackEye offers robust API integration capabilities and webhooks that allow you to connect with your existing tools including CRMs, marketing platforms, and custom applications.

How is the data stored and for how long?

All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. Our standard retention policy keeps data for 12 months, but this is customizable based on your needs and compliance requirements.

Do you offer custom development for specific tracking needs?

Absolutely! Our team can develop custom tracking solutions for unique use cases. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements and we'll create a tailored solution.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 technical support via our Discord community and email. Paid plans include priority support with guaranteed response times. Our knowledge base and documentation are comprehensive and regularly updated.
